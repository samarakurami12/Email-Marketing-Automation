Example Workflows

Prebuilt templates you can use or customize.

Welcome series

  1. Trigger: on_subscribe
  2. Send: Welcome email (immediate)
  3. Wait: 48 hours
  4. Condition: opened?
  5. Action: If not opened → Send reminder

Abandoned cart

  1. Trigger: cart_abandoned (cart id)
  2. Wait: 2 hours – Send first reminder
  3. Wait: 24 hours – Send discount
  4. Action: record conversion → stop

Re-engagement

  1. Trigger: no-open 90 days
  2. Send: Survey / offer
  3. Action: If no response → Move to cold segment
